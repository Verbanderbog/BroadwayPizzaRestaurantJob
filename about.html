About Us

Do you hear the people sing, singing the song of hungry men? Look no further than Broadway Pizza for your next meal! Broadway Pizza serves the best New York style pizza off of Broadway, right here in the Washington DC metroplex.

Interested in online ordering? Check us out on DoorDash!

Customer favorites include the Evita Margherita, Phantom of the Olive, and Meats Me in St. Louis pizzas.

We also offer gluten-free pizzas, a wide variety of burgers and subs, as well as beer and wine.

Catch our daily soups while they're hot, served from 10:30 AM on weekdays until we run out!

And if you're on the go, let us bring a taste of New York, New York to you with our delivery options, take-out options, catering options, and all that jazz!

As a small local business, we're proud to support our local community. Need to feed a crowd? We cater to schools and neighborhood pools!
