About Us

Broadway Pizza serves New York Style pizza at the Cabin John Shopping Center in Potomac, Maryland. Come visit us today!

Come try our popular Gluten Free Pizza!
Our gluten free pizza dough is purchased through the Still Riding Pizza Company.
Our gluten free pizzas are made with special care to prevent cross-contamination, however, wheat flour and other wheat items are still used in our kitchen.

Organizing a school event or non-profit fundraiser?
We want to help! We offer special prices to local schools, pools, and other organizations that benefit our community.
Give us a call or send an e-mail via the Contact section for more details.
